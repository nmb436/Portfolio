Where I've Been

  • University of Missouri

    2007-2011

    At Mizzou, I studied journalism and political science with an empasis on Latin American political systems. These were important years in my life where I learned how to think critically and to express my ideas analytically. I gained experience using quantitative and qualitative researching methods to explore the cross section of democratic transitions, economic development, and monetary policy. I also studied Spanish as a minor.

  • Argentina/Chile

    2010

    In Argentina, I studied Spanish and journalism for a semester at a local university called Universidad Austral. The following summer, I moved to Santiago, Chile, where I continued studying Spanish and earned a certificate of proficiency through the advanced level in reading, writing, and speaking. Most of my time in Chile was spent as a teacher in an adult ESL classroom. It was a formative experience that inspired me to pursue a job as a teacher in a bilingual classroom in Chicago after college.

  • Teaching

    2011-2014

    After college, I spent a year working in AmeriCorps in a Chicago Public School. Next, I decided to become a certified teacher, and spent the next three years teaching 4th grade. My classroom was a bilingual classroom, so I modifed instruction to teach students who spoke English as a second language. During this time, I was also a soccer coach. I coached at my elementary school for two years, and then got a job coaching at a high school at the junior varsity level.

  • Non-Profit Work

    2014-2015

    I worked for a non-profit company in Chicago called First-Slice. First Slice runs a food subscription program, and a network of four cafes with the mission of using the revenue to donate meals to feed the homeless. I was one of the Cafe Managers, and also was on the team where I coordinated the moving of meals to outreach partners, and whole sale clients, as well as daily inventory needed to keep the cafes stocked and running.

  • Software Engineering

    2015-2019

    Currently, I am working as a full stack software developer. Although I'm focused on front end development and web design, I also have worked on projects and written programs using C# and Python. In addition, I have relational dbms experience using SQL Server. I enjoy the work, and look forward to exciting new projects in the future.
